##jsp页面：
1. jstl：	`<%@ taglib prefix="c" uri="http://java.sun.com/jsp/jstl/core" %>`
2. css:		`<link rel="stylesheet" href="<%=basePath%>resources/css/bootstrap.css" />`
3. js:		`<script src="<%=basePath%>resources/js/respond.js"></script>`
